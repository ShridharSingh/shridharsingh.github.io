THREAT 2023 Conference

Presented in "Emerging issues in Cybersecurity - Quantum Computing and AI"

November 20-22, 2023 Shridhar Singh

Topic: "Towards Authenticity of Large-Language Models using Zero-Knowledge Proof Validation"

Shridhar represented the University of KwaZulu-Natal at the THREAT2023 Conference in Stellenbosch, South Africa in November 2023. Drawing attention from the audience in the session titled "Emerging issues in Cybersecurity - Quantumn Computing and AI", Shridhar presented the first academic research paper of the event. His topic "Towards Authenticicty of Large-Language Model Computations using Zero-Knowledge Proof Validations" sparked much interest among the audience and leading to a lengthy panel discussion. Shridhar's paper will be published in January 2024 as part of the Conference Proceedings.
