Bio

MSc Computer Science

Shridhar is a proficient Masters Student at the University of KwaZulu-Natal with a strong focus on achieving academic excellence. Shridhar has demonstrated exceptional leadership capabilities whilst overseeing and directing 3 software projects at the University of KwaZulu-Natal (scoring over 85%) - one of which featured as a showcase project for the University and industry representatives. Shridhar has a proven track record of enhancing team performance, cultivating collaborative excellence among students whilst facilitating effective communication among academic staff and students.

Passionate about learning different programming techniques new to the market, with the ability to absorb complex information, Shridhar creatively implements his ideas based on new technology to projects achieving next-level innovation. Valued with progressive experience in computer science, application support, solutions design, hardware and software troubleshooting and configuration, project management, and system creation and implementation, he translates and implements organisational technical requirements using technology in an effective and ethical manner.
